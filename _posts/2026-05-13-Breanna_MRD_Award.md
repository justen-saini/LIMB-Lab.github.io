Congratulations to our very own Breanna Schneider for winning the 1st-place Dr. Marvin Fritzler Nidus Award for the best poster presentation at McCaig Research Day! Breanna presented the work that she has completed during the first eight months of her Master's work, titled "Exploring the impact of joint hypermobility on knee cartilage using T2 relaxometry".

Way to go Breanna - we are excited to see what you accomplish next!
